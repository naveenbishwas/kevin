About Kevin Missal

Author • Publisher • Visionary

Kevin Missal is not just a bestselling author — he is a storyteller who has built a universe around words, readers, and culture. With multiple imprints under his wing — NuVoice Press, Solomon Press, and Foster Reads — he has sold millions of books across India and created an ecosystem that connects publishers, authors, and readers like never before.

The idea for Kevin Missal Book Café came from a simple thought: What if books could be experienced the way coffee is — savored, shared, and celebrated?

Today, each café is designed as a reader’s living room. Walls lined with timeless classics, exclusive titles from Kevin’s publishing houses, a carefully curated coffee menu, and events that make literature come alive.

The café is more than just a business — it’s an extension of Kevin’s journey as a writer. A space where every visitor is welcomed as a reader first, and every franchise partner becomes part of a movement that puts stories back at the heart of everyday life.
